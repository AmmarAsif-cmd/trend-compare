## How It Works

### 1. Generation Process

```
Trending Comparisons â†’ AI Analysis â†’ Blog Post Draft â†’ Your Approval â†’ Published
```

**What the AI generates:**
- Title (SEO-optimized, 60-70 chars)
- Excerpt (compelling summary, 150-160 chars)
- Content (800-1500 words in Markdown)
- Meta title & description
- Keywords & tags
- Category & read time

### 2. Content Types

The AI can generate 4 types of posts:

1. **Comparison Analysis** - "iPhone vs Android: Trend Insights"
2. **Trend Analysis** - "The Rise of AI Search Tools"
3. **Category Roundup** - "Top Tech Trends of 2024"
4. **Seasonal** - "Holiday Shopping Trends Analysis"

### 3. Approval Workflow

```
draft â†’ pending_review â†’ approved â†’ published
```

- `pending_review`: Generated by AI, needs your review
- `approved`: You approved it, ready to publish
- `published`: Live on your blog
- `rejected`: Not suitable, deleted

## Pro Tips

### 1. Edit Before Publishing
The AI is good but not perfect. Always:
- Check facts and dates
- Add your personal insights
- Fix any awkward phrasing
- Ensure brand voice consistency

### 2. Pick Your Best
Don't publish everything. Only approve:
- Posts with unique insights
- Topics with search volume
- Content you'd be proud to share

### 3. Batch Generate, Slow Publish
- Generate 20 posts at once
- Publish 2-3 per week
- Keeps your blog active without overwhelming readers

### 4. Track Performance
After 1 month:
- Which categories get most views?
- Which posts lead to comparison clicks?
- Which sources bring traffic?

Then generate MORE of what works.

## Troubleshooting

### "No posts generated"
**Cause**: No recent comparisons in database
**Fix**: Wait for users to create comparisons, or manually create seed data

### "AI generation failed"
**Cause**: Missing ANTHROPIC_API_KEY
**Fix**: Add to your `.env` file:
```bash
ANTHROPIC_API_KEY=sk-ant-...
```

### "Can't access admin dashboard"
**Cause**: Route not configured
**Fix**: Add authentication later. For now it's open.

### "Markdown not rendering"
**Cause**: react-markdown not installed
**Fix**: `npm install react-markdown` (already done)
