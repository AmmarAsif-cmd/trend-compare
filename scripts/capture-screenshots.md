## Quick Capture Guide

### Method 1: Browser DevTools (Recommended)

#### Chrome/Edge:
1. Open your site: `http://localhost:3000`
2. Press `F12` to open DevTools
3. Press `Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + P` (Command Palette)
4. Type: `Capture full size screenshot`
5. Press Enter
6. Screenshot saved to Downloads folder

#### Firefox:
1. Open your site: `http://localhost:3000`
2. Press `F12` to open DevTools
3. Click Settings (gear icon)
4. Go to "Screenshot" section
5. Click "Capture full page"
6. Screenshot saved

### Method 2: Browser Extension

**Recommended Extensions:**
- **Full Page Screen Capture** (Chrome)
- **FireShot** (Chrome/Firefox)
- **Awesome Screenshot** (Chrome/Firefox)

### Method 3: Built-in Tools

**Windows:**
- Snipping Tool (Windows 10)
- Snip & Sketch (Windows 10+)
- ShareX (Free, powerful)

**Mac:**
- Cmd + Shift + 4 (Area)
- Cmd + Shift + 3 (Full screen)

## Image Specifications

- **Width:** Minimum 1200px (1920px recommended)
- **Format:** PNG (preferred) or JPG
- **Size:** Under 5MB per image
- **Aspect:** Landscape (16:9 or 4:3)

---

## Editing Checklist

After capturing:

1. **Crop** - Remove browser UI
2. **Resize** - Ensure 1200px+ width
3. **Optimize** - Compress if needed
4. **Review** - Check for clarity
5. **Save** - Use descriptive names
